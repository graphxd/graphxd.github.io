Graphs Across Domains
=====================

GraphXD connects scientists, researchers, and theorists interested in graphs
from a variety of fields.
Inspired by `TextXD <https://bids.berkeley.edu/research/textxd>`_ and `ImageXD
<https://bids.berkeley.edu/research/image-xd>`_, GraphXD is organized by
`Berkeley Institute for Data Science (BIDS) <https://bids.berkeley.edu>`_
fellows and is open to the campus community.
