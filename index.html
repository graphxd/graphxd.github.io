Graphs Across Domains¶

GraphXD connects scientists, researchers, and theorists interested in graphs from a variety of fields.

Inspired by TextXD and ImageXD, GraphXD is being developed by fellows at UC Berkeley’s Institute for Data Science (BIDS). We plan to form a core group of 10 to 20 members from across the Berkeley campus, which will meet regularly starting during the fall semester. We have a tentative plan to host an inaugural workshop at BIDS prior to the start of the spring semester (possibly January 10–12, 2018). The inaugural workshop will include speakers and participants from outside Berkeley.

Additional details will be posted here soon.
