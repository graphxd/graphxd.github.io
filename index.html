Graphs Across Domains¶

Graphs (or networks) are composed of vertices connected by edges. They arise in many fields including mathematics, computer science, biology, physics, economics, and sociology. GraphXD connects scientists, researchers, and theorists interested in graphs from a variety of fields through seminars and workshops.

Inspired by TextXD and ImageXD, GraphXD is organized by Berkeley Institute for Data Science (BIDS) fellows and is open to the campus community.
